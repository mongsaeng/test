Master your English,
Master your future. Every G-TELP test is designed with your needs and future in mind. Here at G-TELP,
we provide a comprehensive assessment of your English language abilities so that you’re well-equipped for navigating your future careers. Whether at home or abroad, new or advanced, we are here to lead you on your journey to mastery.

Take reliable tests
anytime, anywhere
Experience the convenience and flexibility of taking the G-TELP test anytime, anywhere. Whether you prefer to take the test at a designated center or from the comfort of your own home, we've got you covered.

Plus, with our innovative and reliable AI-based proctoring service, you can trust that your test will be monitored fairly and accurately.
Save money and choose the skills
YOU want to test
Maximize your time and money with our flexible test options.
From speaking and writing or reading and listening, you can take the section of the test you need to suit your schedule and goals.
Used by thousands of institutions, schools and companies around the world
Join the rapidly growing ranks of millions of successful G-TELP test takers from 210+ countries around the world and take control of your future. With G-TELP scores accepted for college admissions, job applications, promotions, and more, you can open up a world of opportunities.

Trusted and proven step-by-step approach to fluency

Become a proficient English speaker with G-TELP's proven, step-by-step approach.
With five levels of proficiency, you can follow our curriculum from level 5 to level 1 and watch your fluency grow at each stage.
