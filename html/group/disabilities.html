Home > ITSC Group > Disabilities

ITSC strives to ensure that our test is impartial and accessible to all. Depending on individual circumstances, accommodations such as extra time, adjusted proctoring, or external aids may be granted. These accommodations are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. If you require accommodations for the test, please complete the form provided. Make sure you obtain approval for your accommodations from ITSCâ€™s Disability Services by submitting your request prior to scheduling your test.
Information related to an individual's diagnosis or medical details will not be disclosed by ITSC unless they have given their informed written consent or it is mandated by legal proceedings. Any information that is released will be done so on a "need-to-know" basis, unless there is a legal requirement to do otherwise. Moreover, to protect the privacy of people with disabilities, assessors may hold back or remove any part of the documentation that is not directly pertinent to ITSC's standards for determining a disability as outlined by the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), and a rationale for the requested testing accommodations.

In addition, ITSC recommends that all institutions provide secure storage facilities for confidential and sensitive student and employee information, which could be used to support requests for ITSC accommodations.
