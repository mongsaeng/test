Sample Test

"Maximize the effectiveness of your G-TELP exam preparation with sample tests. These mock tests are designed with the same question format as the actual exam, allowing for unlimited practice and preparation under conditions similar to the actual exam. Through each sample test, you can easily identify the types of questions you may encounter on the day of your test."

G-TELP Level Sample Test The questions are presented in a multiple-choice format and are designed to evaluate grammar, listening comprehension, reading skills, and vocabulary knowledge.
