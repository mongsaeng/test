Join us to become one of our Test Centers

Work together with G-TELP
We appreciate your interest in becoming a certified G-TELP Test Center. These centers are esteemed institutions equipped with secure testing facilities where G-TELP exams are administered to individuals taking the test. As certified centers, they provide their facilities for conducting exams and also manage appointment scheduling for test-takers. Start your application process today to become a certified G-TELP Test Center.
If you want to work with us, please click here to provide your contact information.

Frequently asked questions

Who is eligible to become a certified G-TELP Test Center?

Our Certified G-TELP Test Centers encompass a diverse range of backgrounds. Typically, we encourage academic and testing facilities that possess a dependable computer lab, suitable facilities, and a secure network to apply. By becoming a Certified Test Center, you will have the opportunity to serve the public and will be responsible for scheduling testing dates while adhering to G-TELP's security policies. This entails utilizing a webcam and ensuring proctored testing is conducted as required.

How do Test centers benefit from being a partner with G-TELP?

Institutions stand to gain financial and professional advantages by providing test-takers with greater accessibility to G-TELP. Here are some of the benefits associated with becoming a Test Center:
1. Drawing clients to your testing site
2. Expanding your international exposure
3. Receiving marketing spotlights from G-TELP
4. Joining the extensive G-TELP networking community
5. Generating profits and much more!

How can our test centers join the partnership?

Becoming a certified G-TELP Test Center is a simple process. Just complete our Test Center Application Form and submit it online. One of our representatives will then respond to you and provide guidance throughout the approval process. If you have any inquiries about requirements and costs, please feel free to reach out to us.

What kind of technical requirements need to be met for a test center?

The technical requirements for a test center vary based on the type of testing:
1. Paper-Based Testing:
- Desks: Each testing room must have at least 20 desks to accommodate examinees.
- Broadcasting System: A broadcasting system is required to communicate announcements or instructions to all examinees simultaneously
2. Computer-Based Testing:
- Computers: Each testing room should have at lease 10 computers available for examinees.
- Internet Access: All computers must have a stable internet connection to ensure a smooth testing experience.
- Equipment: All computers must be equipped with a headphone and microphone.
