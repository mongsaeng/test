Our Program

Collage Admissions Counseling
We specialize in ensuring your college application shines. Our team assists students in:
  • Crafting compelling essays that capture your unique story.
  • Mastering interviews with confidence and clarity.
  • Navigating the complexities of transfer applications.
  • Strategizing for success with Advanced Placement.
Impact School
Our Impact School offers a range of activities tailored to shape students’ futures and get them where they want to be.
English Camps
We host an array of English camps tailored to diverse learning objectives, including : Comprehensive English language camps.
  • Intensive boot camp sessions.
  • STEM-focused English camps to merge language learning with technical expertise.
ECS (extracurriculars)
Our dedicated team designs and supports extracurricular initiatives centered on social impact. By engaging in these transformative activities, students will broaden their horizons and gain valuable experience for their application essays, giving them an edge in the admissions process.
Internship Assistance
Unlock real-world experiences with G-TELP's professional network. We bridge the gap between academia and the corporate world, helping students gain invaluable on-the-ground experience in reputable companies.
