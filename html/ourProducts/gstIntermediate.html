GST Intermediate

Course Description
Developing the skills and strategies necessary to perform your best on the GST(G-TELP Speaking Test)
Working on activities that correspond directly to the test in speaking skill
Course Objectives
Level 5 or higher in G-TELP Speaking Test
Target Audience
Over basic level of English
Curriculum
Curriculum
Learning Time Contents
0 20 Orientation
1 20 Part 1. Giving personal information
2 20 Part 2. Describing a familiar setting/objects

4 Part 4. Narrating a story from pictures
