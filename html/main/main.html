Product Brand

The G-TELP is an internationally recognized English proficiency test devised by a distinguished team of professors, esteemed linguists and evaluation experts from the International Testing Institute. Respected around the world.

G-TELP
Speaking Test

G-TELP Speaking Test is a measure of general English language oral proficiency.
Speaking

G-TELP
Writing Test

G-TELP Writing Test is a measure of general English language writing proficiency.
Writing

G-TELP
Level Test

The General Tests of English Language Proficiency assess English at five different levels of functional ability.
Level

G-TELP Business
Speaking Test

The G-TELP Business Speaking Test assesses an examinee’s ability to use English to communicate in a business setting.
Business Speaking

G-TELP Business
Writing Test

The G-TELP Business Writing Test assesses an examinee’s ability to use English to communicate in a business setting.
Business Writing

G-TELP
Junior Test

G-TELP Junior Tests assess the English language proficiency of young students (ages 6 to 13) at five different levels.
Junior

G-TELP IBT Home Edition

ITSC (International Testing Services Center) is deeply committed to improving English assessment(s) and other related issues by providing full support to rigorous research studies and projects on English Linguistic and Education, academic publishing, international academic exchanges, and other methods of scholarly communication. We aim to give diverse and rich opportunities to all scholars and researchers to expand their horizons in mapping, evaluating and advancing high-quality English assessments and measurements both nationwide and worldwide.
