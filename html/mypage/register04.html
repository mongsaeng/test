User Information Required

Your Identification (ID) Requirements for the G-TELP tests depend on where you plan to test and your country of citizenship. We strongly encourage you to provide your Passport or National ID number during registration, rather than any other type of ID.

  • If you plan to study. Work, or settle in Australla or the United Kingdom, you must provide a Passport or National ID, required by the immigration authorities in those countries.
  • Enter letters and numbers only – no special characters, hyphens or spaces. Example : **12345 – enter as. 12345
  • You are required to enter one ID before you can register for a test.*

IMPORTANT Your Korean National ID cannot be used as your primary ID when you register for the TOEFL test. If you are a citizen of and testing within the Republic of Korea, your National ID will be accepted if you present it at the test center, but when you register you must use a different ID.

Enter your passport, if you have one – or use the “Other” field to enter your driver’s license or another acceptable form of ID. Do not enter your National ID in the “Other” field.

PASSPORT

If you don’t have the preferred identification (Passport or Nation ID), then provide a Driver’s Licence or Other ID below that meets the identification (ID) Requirements for the country/location where you plan to test.
