The following information applies to those who will be taking a G-TELP Level Test or G-TELP Junior test at a test center.

ID Requirements and Test Procedures

Before you take the test, please review the ID Requirements and familiarize yourself with the Bulletin, where you can find useful information about test procedures, item restrictions, and check-in details.

Identification (ID) Requirements

Your ITSC account name and the name on your ID must match

When setting up your ITSC account and registering for your exam, you must ensure that the name on your account matches exactly with the name on your ID card. Any discrepancies between these names could render you ineligible to take the test, with no refund of your exam fee. If you anticipate potential discrepancies, it's advisable to contact G-TELP Services before finalizing your registration. Furthermore, should your name change after registration, you can seek assistance from G-TELP Services.

Important information about test security and test-taker conduct

Every test center must adhere to specific policies and procedures to ensure a fair and secure testing environment for all. When at a test center, you are required to follow the instructions provided by the test center staff. Failure to do so may result in you being denied the chance to take the test, with no refund for your test fee. This rule applies to your behavior before, during, and after the test, including any breaks. Any infractions could lead to your dismissal from the test center and/or the cancellation of your test scores.

We strongly recommend that you review all security policies and procedures prior to your test. These can be found in our bulletin.
