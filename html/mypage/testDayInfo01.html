Identification (ID) Requirements

Your ITSC account name and the name on your ID must match

When setting up your ITSC account and registering for your exam, you must ensure that the name on your account matches exactly with the name on your ID card. Any discrepancies between these names could render you ineligible to take the test, with no refund of your exam fee. If you anticipate potential discrepancies, it's advisable to contact G-TELP Services before finalizing your registration. Furthermore, should your name change after registration, you can seek assistance from G-TELP Services.

Important information about test security and test-taker conduct

During a G-TELP at Home test, you will be guided by a proctor who must adhere to stringent policies and procedures to ensure a fair and secure test environment. It is crucial that you follow the instructions provided by the proctor or those displayed on-screen during the test. Failure to do so may result in you being denied the opportunity to take the test, with no refund on the test fee. This rule applies to your conduct before, during, and after the test, including any breaks. Any violations may lead to your dismissal from the online test room and/or invalidation of your test scores. Please ensure you review all the security policies and procedures before your test. These can be found in our bulletin.
