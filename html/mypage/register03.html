Review ID Requirements

  • All test takers must provide valid, acceptable identification (ID) documents to be admitted to a test center. Your ID requirements depend on your country of citizenship and where you plan to test. Review ID requirements This link will open in a new window for the country where you plan to test. If you do not contact OTI and are unable to test or your scores are held or canceled because you do not have acceptable ID, your test fee will not be refunded.
