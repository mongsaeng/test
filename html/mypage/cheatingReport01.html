Cheating Report

G-TELP KOREA has instituted a Misconduct Reporting Center to uphold the fairness and integrity of our English proficiency examinations.

We encourage individuals to report any misconduct, irregularities, or unfair practices related to the G-TELP examination, supported by tangible evidence. Misconduct may encompass activities such as identity impersonation, tampering with test materials, illegal capture of test content, use of unauthorized communication devices during the test, or any organized unfair practices.

In submitting a report, we request that you provide comprehensive information rooted in facts, upholding principles of accuracy, honesty, and impartiality, backed by clear evidence. Please note that deliberate or malicious false reporting can lead to legal consequences under applicable laws and regulations.

The Misconduct Reporting Center operates under a real-name policy, assuring strict confidentiality protection for the reporter.

For general questions or grievances concerning the examination, including appeals, please reach us at 1588-0589.
We are here to assist and ensure a fair testing environment.

Scope of reporting

Peeking at the test paper or answer sheet of others
Impersonation with forged/altered ID
Leaking test questions through photography or recording
Any electronic sound during the test
Organized misconduct (providing answers to others or similar behavior)
Other suspected violations of regulations
