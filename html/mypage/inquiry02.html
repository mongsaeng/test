1. Personal Information We Collect

G-TELP KOREA is committed to limiting the collection of personal information to the bare minimum necessary for the provision of our services. name, contact information, email

2. Purpose of collection and Use of Personal Information:

  • name: Your name is collected as basic identification data to appropriately address and respond to your inquiries.
  • contact information: Your contact details are collected to establish a reliable channel of communication for responding to your queries and concerns.
  • Email: Your email address is collected to ensure a dependable method of communication for addressing and responding to your inquiries.

3. Retention and Use of Personal Information

G-TELP KOREA follows a principle of promptly deleting personal information once the purpose of its collection or receipt is achieved. This ensures that your information is not stored for a longer duration than is necessary. However, there are circumstances where we may be legally required to retain your personal information even after the purpose of its collection has been fulfilled. This is to comply with legal regulations and may include situations such as resolving consumer complaints or disputes.
records relating to consumer complaints or dispute resolutions are retained for a period of 3 years.
