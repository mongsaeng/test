My Score

1) Score release date
- G-TELP Level : 5 business days after the test
- G-TELP Speaking : 7 business days after the test
2) Regulations and number of free score reports
- Initial Score Report is free.
- Additional score reports cost $20 each.
3) Score review
- Within 30 days after the test
- Score review is available only for the speaking and writing sections (GST, GWT, GBST, GBWT)
- Score review request is available only once
- Score review request is not accepted if scores have already been sent to other institutions or schools
- $80 per each section
- The original scores will not remain valid while the score review is in process.
- The score review fee will be refunded when the reviewed scores differ from the original scores.
4) A grade presentation examination
- G-TELP Level Test, G-TELP Junior Test
5) Validity of score report
- The score report will be available for up to 2 years from the test day, and the score will not be available after the given validation date.
